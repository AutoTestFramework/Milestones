    Hotjar

  • Using only one platform (Hotjar), we created the feedback form & we simulated the user behavior in order to improve the user experience - Google Analytics doesn't provide a feedback form.
  • The platform showed us how our visitors are really using the pages themselves. This allowed us to quickly identify opportunities for improvement and growth.

    • Google Analytics

  • Google Analytics was used only to give data about the pages (which page has the highest bounce rate or conversion rate).

    • Combining both platforms, we used Google Analytics to report why our homepage has a very high bounce rate. Hotjar was used to show us why that is happening, using Heatmaps, recordings and polls. This allowed us to make the changes needed to decrease bounce rate.

    Google Analytics and HotJar: the winning combination

  • We find out when the user clicks + stays + navigates (HotJar) and converts (Google Analytics).

    Newsletter

  • After launching the subscription option, we saw a positive feedback from our potential customers and theirs needs to subscribe. They liked our business plan: 14 days free for testing the application, having 24/7 support from us. The price (Price package for ATF) for our product didn't seem to bother because we received lots of comments about the release date of our product.

    • Facebook

  • Most of the traffic came from posts on Facebook. We created also a page for our project (Atf's page on Facebook) in order to see if we can get subscribers. It was also important for us to be visible on strong social network that can gave us the future potential customers. We put time and effort in order to reach potential customers. We asked for feedback because we needed validation for the application (what are the strong points for the current application). Every customer that came up for discussion received a personal note that explained what are the next steps for the project.

    • Hotjar and Google Analytics

  • We saw that people didn't stayed too much on our landing page. It's important to have the main things on top of the page: PRICE & SUBSCRIBE BUTTON. For preventing people to leave the page earlier, we adjusted the page. After we analized how much they spend on our page, where they navigates, we remade the landing page in order to help people to find the requirements for our product better.
